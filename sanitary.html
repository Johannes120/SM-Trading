Specialized Sanitary Installations

Our comprehensive sanitary services range from hygienic dispenser installations to high-quality waste management solutions, ensuring a clean and professional environment for your facilities.

Hand Hygiene

Hand Soap Dispensers

We provide professional installation of wall-mounted hand soap dispensers. These units are designed for durability and ease of use, ensuring consistent hygiene standards in both commercial and public restrooms.

Restroom Care

Sanitary Urinal Mats

Our premium urinal mats are strategically placed to manage odors and maintain cleanliness. These mats are essential for preventing floor damage and ensuring a fresh-smelling environment in high-traffic facilities.

Waste Management

Shebin Sanitary Bins

We install and maintain high-quality shebins (feminine hygiene bins). These bins are designed for discreet, hands-free operation and are serviced regularly to ensure the highest levels of workplace sanitation.

Hand Drying

Paper Towel Dispensers

Professional mounting of high-capacity paper towel dispensers. Our installations prioritize accessibility and waste reduction, providing a reliable and hygienic drying solution for all staff and visitors.

Maintenance

Hardware Maintenance

Routine inspection of dispenser mechanisms to ensure non-clogging and smooth operation. We verify that all internal components of your soap and paper units are functioning at peak performance.

Replenishment

Consumable Replenishment

Part of our sanitary service includes the consistent restocking of soaps and paper products. We ensure your facility never runs out of the essential supplies required for daily hygiene.

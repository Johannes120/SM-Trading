General Facility Maintenance

From structural reinforcement to component restoration, we provide comprehensive maintenance services to keep your property efficient and safe.

Structural Repair

Metallic Hardware Restoration

We maintain high-traffic metallic fixtures by removing oxidation and applying protective coatings. This process prevents rust and ensures the structural integrity of the component for long-term use.

Refurbishment

Wall-Mounted Unit Refurbishment

Our maintenance team inspects mounted facility units to ensure they are securely fastened to the substrate. We clean and polish the casing to maintain a professional appearance while checking for any hairline fractures in the housing.

Drainage Care

Floor-Level Component Maintenance

To maintain floor-level drainage and filtration components, we perform deep-cleansing flushes to clear debris. This prevents blockages and ensures that all liquids flow correctly without damaging the surrounding floor finish.

System Calibration

Facility Dispensing Maintenance

We maintain large-scale dispensing units by calibrating the internal release mechanisms. This ensures consistent operation and prevents mechanical jamming, extending the lifespan of the facility's equipment.

Safety Check

Structural Anchor Inspection

We maintain the safety of wall-mounted hardware by inspecting the tension of anchor bolts. We ensure there is no "play" in the movement, preventing the unit from detaching and causing injury or property damage.

Preventative Care

Tiling Eruption Maintenance

Our maintenance includes the inspection of tiles as time goes by. We remove old, failing tiling and replace it to prevent moisture ingress into the building structure.
